Mage

🧑

Previously displayed with a gendered appearance, now shown as gender neutral on most platforms.

A person who wields magical powers derived from supernatural or occult sources.

Mage was approved as part of Unicode 10.0 in 2017 and added to Emoji 5.0 in 2017.
